ABOUT US

Wave Foundation is a management and creative music company founded in 2021, Built on strategy, taste, and execution, Wave Foundation has become a key force in shaping the music culture - especially within the Persian music scene, where our influence and blueprint are widely adopted.

Our company sits at the intersection of music, business, and creativity - managing talent, building brands, and helping artists scale their vision globally. Whether it's strategy, creative direction, or business development, we operate with a hands-on, full-picture approach that adapts to each artist’s individual needs.

Our roster reflects who we are: sonically diverse and globally minded. We manage artists like Nina Woods, an R&B artist and instrumentalist bringing live elements into the commercial space; Pressa and Bundog, two of Canada’s most recognizable rap names, whose label Blue Feathers Records (BFR) we help run through a strategic partnership focused on operations and creative rollout; and Pouria K and Mersad, Persian artists leading a new wave in the culture and sound of today’s Persian R&B and rap.

The name Wave Foundation speaks to our mission: every wave, every movement, trend, or sound, needs something solid behind it. That’s what we are: A foundation built to last, pushing the culture forward.
